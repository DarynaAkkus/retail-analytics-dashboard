# Retail Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

Dashboard focused on retail performance across product lines,
locations, and customer segments.

## Tools & Technologies

* **IBM Cognos Analytics**
* **Data Visualization**

## Key Findings

* Revenue exceeds $2.7M with stable margin distribution
* Electronics, gaming, and TV categories generate the highest revenue
* Major cities like London and Toronto lead in sales volume
* Customer loyalty is evenly distributed across tiers (Gold, Silver, VIP, etc.)
* Revenue trends vary across years and product categories
